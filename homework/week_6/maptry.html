The distribution of world population and the division of religionous believes over the world
Daphne Box, data-sets come from Kaggle





The number of inhabitants and distribution of religions vary widely between countries. It is therefore usually hard to estimate how religionous believes are divided over the world. Therefore the number of inhabitants per country are shown on this webpage via a color-coded system. When a country is clicked the division of the five main religions and percentage of irreligious individuals is shown. Since the relative numbers of the religious groups present per country has been shifting a lot the last couple of years, due to many reasons. The data is shown for 1960 and 2010.
